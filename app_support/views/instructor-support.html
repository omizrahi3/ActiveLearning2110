Login and Register Account

  • Enter your email address you signed up with. This should be your Georgia Tech email address.
  • Enter your password you signed up with.
  • Click Login
  • If you don't have an account, Contact admin for an Instructor Key. This key will allow you to create an account in instructor priviledges.
  • Enter your first and last name and Georgia Tech email address and then choose a password.
  • Click on the Professor Button to enable the text field and then enter the Instructor Key you were given by admin.
  • Click on Register

Edit Profile

  • Click your name in the top-left to open the Menu.
  • Select Profile
  • Press Edit Profile at the bottom of the page.
  • Make edits and press Save Changes when you are done.
To change your profile picture, press the image under Edit Profile and file manager will pop up.

Manage Passwords

  • Click your name in the top-left to open the menu.
  • Select Profile.
  • Press Edit Profile at the bottom of the page.
  • Enter your Old Password, the one that is to be changed.
  • Enter your New Password.
  • Press Save Changes located on the bottom of the page.

Sign Out of an Account

  • Click your name in the top-left to open the menu
  • Select Logout.

Create a Course

  • Press the Create Course button located on the left side of the menu or the [+] button located on the top of your dashboard.
  • Enter class type and class number.
  • Enter class days, class time, and the semester the class is offered.
  • Enter section numbers.
  • Click Create.
You can enter multiple sections at once. Type out all sections in the Add Sections text box. Press enter after each section.

Add Students

  • Under the course section, to retrieve the Student Key for students to join the section, press the clipboard icon on the top-right corner of the section card.
  • Alternatively, to add a group of students at once via .csv or to manage students, press Manage Students on the bottom of the section card.
  • On top of the page, select Add.
  • Click Choose CSV and select .csv file with students.
  • Click Submit.
The CSV file of students can directly be pulled from the TSquare website.

Create a Question

  • On the sidebar menu to the left, click on Create Question.
  • To edit the question, Select pen icon on the top left.
  • Click on portion of the question you want to edit to edit.
  • Click on the check icon to save edits.
In order to change the question to multiple choice from True/False, press the toggle on the top left. To set the correct answer choice, press the check icon next to the corresponding answer choice.

Create a Daily Lecture

  • From the dashboard, make sure you go into a course you want to create the lecture under.
  • In the sidebar menu to the left, click on Create Lecture.
  • Enter a title and date.
  • Under Lectures, you should now see the lecture you have just created.
  • In order to add questions to lectures you have created, look into the list of lectures. Find the lecture you want to edit on the list.
  • Press corresponding pen icon to the right.
  • In the text box, look up the question you want to include in the lecture.
  • Select the question and press Add Question right of the text box.

Start/End a Daily Lecture

  • Under your course, look under Lectures section and find the lecture you want to start.
  • Select the Play button.
  • From the dropdown menu on the top of the page, select the question you want to start.
  • To adjust the time a student has on the question, adjust using + to add time or - to deduct time.
  • To end a daily lecture, simply exit out of the lecture by going back to the course section page.

Review Student Progress

  • As students answer the question during a lecture session, a results graph will be populated on screen.
