Login and Register Account

  • Enter your email address you signed up with and password.
  • Click Login
  • If you don't have an account, press Register
  • Enter your first and last name and Georgia Tech email address and then choose a password.
Note that as a student, you don't need a Professor Key so you may ignore that field and press Register when required fields are complete.

Edit Profile

  • Click your name in the top-left to open the Menu.
  • Select Profile
  • Press Edit Profile at the bottom of the page.
  • Make edits and press Save Changes when you are done.
To change your profile picture, press the image under Edit Profile and file manager will pop up.

Manage Passwords

  • Click your name in the top-left to open the menu.
  • Select Profile.
  • Press Edit Profile at the bottom of the page.
  • Enter your Old Password, the one that is to be changed.
  • Enter your New Password.
  • Press Save Changes located on the bottom of the page.

Sign Out of an Account

  • Click your name in the top-left to open the menu
  • Select Logout.

Join Course

  • Press the Join Course button located on the left side of the menu or the [+] button located on the top of your dashboard.
  • Enter the Section key provided by your instructor.
  • Click Join.
  • Upon successful join, course will appear on your dashboard. Press View course to enter into course lectures.

Join a Daily Lecture

  • From your dashboard, View Course.
  • Navigate into the Lectures section.
  • Press Join next to the daily lecture.
Once you are joined, you will wait on your professor to start a question. The question will automatically appear on screen.

Answering Questions

  • Select the radio button with the correct answer to the question.
  • Click Submit. Keep in mind that answering the question is timed according to the timer in the top right.
Note that once you submit an answer, you will not be able to answer the question again. Once you submit, your answer marked in green will indicate that the answer is correct - red if the answer is not. Your grade is indicated on the top left.
